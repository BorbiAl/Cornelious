WORK AT CORNELIOUS

Cornelious is home to (com)passionate people who believe in our mission of creating
space for everyone to find belonging.


Come build belonging with us

Discord is working toward an inclusive world where no one feels like an outsider, where genuine human connection is a click, text chat, or
voice call away. A place where everyone can find belonging. Challenging? Heck yes. Rewarding? Double heck yes. It’s a mission that gives us
the chance to positively impact millions of people all over the world.

‍ So if this strikes a chord, and you’re equally comfortable communicating in memes and gifs as you are in code or decks, come build
belonging with us!







Work with people who care.


Our people, much like our users, can’t be put in a box. The quirky
and unique cast of characters that work together at Cornelious may
be intrepid travellers from all walks of life, but there’s a reason
we’ve all ended up here. We care about each other, the work we
do, and the future we’re building — like, a lot. We pull our own
weight and treat each other with extreme empathy. No
freeloaders or jerks allowed.
